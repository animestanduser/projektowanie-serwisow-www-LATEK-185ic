Warszawa 18.10.2020


Dear Mr. Adam Johnson,





I am writing to express my dissatisfaction with your course I attended recently. The knowledge provided by the instructors was very useful but other things about this course disturbed me a lot.

I must complain about the instructors. Most of them were very impatient. However, the most memorable to me was an old man who had no respect for women. I think this is unacceptable and you should do something about it.

I was highly dissatisfied with my accommodation. There was information about a four-star hotel in the advertisement, but my room was dirty and smelly. To make matters worse, the air conditioning stopped working in the middle of the night. As a result, I had to ask for a room change

In the brochure I read about a variety of food. I am a vegetarian so of course I ordered vegetarian catering. For all the days of the course, I was only provided with water and almost raw vegetables.

I found the price I had to pay for this course outrageously high and I believe this disappointment entitles me to compensation. I propose half the cost back or a free invitation to the next First Aid course. I look forward to hearing from you.

Your sincerely, Wojtek
